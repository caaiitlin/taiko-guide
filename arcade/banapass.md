# Bandai Namco Passport 

Found an arcade? The next step is getting a Bandai Namco Passport, commonly referred to as a **Banapass**. This card lets you save your progress, customise your profile, participate in online events, and access many of the online features available in the arcade version of Taiko no Tatsujin.
